Our mission and vision

Weather and Disaster is a web application designed to educate users on natural disaster mainly flood, cyclone and earthquake. Furthermore, it provides weather information including a 7 day forecast for any location of your choice

CYCLONE

Cyclones can be the most intense storms on Earth. A cyclone is a system of winds rotating counterclockwise in the Northern Hemisphere around a low pressure center.

FLOOD

A Flood is an overflow of water that submerges land that is usually dry. Floods are an area of study in the discipline of hydrology. They are the most common and widespread natural severe weather event.
